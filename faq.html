How long does it take to build a custom tiny home?
Typical timeline is 12-16 weeks from design approval to completion. This includes custom cabinetry, electrical, plumbing, and final inspections. We prioritize quality without unnecessary delays.
Do I need a permit for a tiny home?
Permit requirements vary by location. Our team assists with zoning research and can connect you with local experts. Most tiny homes on wheels are classified as RVs, while foundation-built homes follow standard residential codes.
What's the average cost of a TinyCraft home?
Our models range from $49,900 to $89,500 depending on size, finishes, and off-grid features. Custom builds start at $65,000. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
Do you offer financing options?
Yes! We partner with specialized tiny home lenders offering RV loans, personal loans, and construction-to-permanent financing. Our team can guide you through pre-approval and payment plans.
Where can I park my tiny home?
Options include private land, tiny home communities, RV parks, and backyard dwellings. We provide a directory of tiny-friendly locations and can help with zoning research in your area.
Can a tiny home accommodate a family?
Absolutely! Our larger models (400-600 sq ft) feature multiple lofts, bunk rooms, and clever storage. We specialize in family-friendly layouts with space for 2-4 people comfortably.
Are TinyCraft homes eco-friendly?
Yes! We use sustainable timber, low-VOC paints, energy-efficient appliances, and solar-ready electrical. Many of our homes achieve net-zero energy use with optional solar packages.
Can I go off-grid with my tiny home?
Definitely! We offer off-grid packages including solar panels, battery storage, composting toilets, and rainwater collection systems. Perfect for remote locations and sustainable living enthusiasts.
Do you deliver tiny homes nationwide?
Yes! We deliver to all 50 states. Our logistics team handles transportation permits, route planning, and on-site placement. Delivery costs vary by distance.
